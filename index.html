What is a Hydraulic Press?

A hydraulic press is a machine that uses liquid pressure to multiply force. It works on Pascal’s law and is widely used in industries, vehicles, and machines.

Hydraulic Press Facts

Scientific Facts

  • Works on Pascal’s Law
  • Uses incompressible fluids
  • Multiplies force
  • Used in car lifts and factories
Hydraulic Press Experiment

Syringe Hydraulic Press Experiment

Connect two syringes of different sizes with a tube filled with water. Press the smaller syringe to see force multiplication.
