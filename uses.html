Common Uses of Hydraulic Presses

  • Forging, clinching, molding, blanking, punching, and deep drawing.
  • Metal forming in automotive (body panels, brake pads) and aerospace.
  • Assembly and disassembly of tight components.
  • Straightening bent parts, separating rusted items, crushing oil filters.
  • Farm equipment repair (pressing bearings).
  • Waste processing (garbage trucks, car crushers).
  • Powder compacting for shapes and designs.
  • Shaping sheet metal (kitchen sinks, car panels).
  • Broaching keyways, re-sleeving valves.
  • Pressing auto parts (bearings, bushings, U-joints).
